New York, USA

New York City is a bustling metropolis known for its iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and Central Park. Experience the vibrant culture, world-class dining, and endless entertainment options in the city that never sleeps.

Top Attractions

Discover the must-see attractions in each destination.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom and democracy, located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. Visitors can tour the monument and enjoy panoramic views of the city skyline and the Statue of Liberty Museum.

Accommodations

The Plaza Hotel

The Plaza Hotel is a historic landmark located in Midtown Manhattan, offering luxury accommodations, fine dining, and unparalleled service. Experience the grandeur of New York City from this iconic hotel.

Activities

Central Park Bike Tour

Explore the iconic Central Park in New York City, USA, on a guided bike tour. Cycle through lush greenery, past scenic ponds, and famous landmarks while learning about the park's history and hidden gems from knowledgeable guides.

Travel Tips

Helpful tips and advice for a smooth travel experience.

Pack Light

Traveling with less luggage makes it easier to move around and reduces the risk of lost or stolen items. Pack only the essentials and versatile clothing items that you can mix and match.
